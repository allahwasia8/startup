Post Office Liverpool: Branches, Opening Times & Essential Services

Whether you're posting a parcel, exchanging travel money or picking up a missed delivery, knowing where to find your nearest post office in Liverpool saves time and hassle. This guide covers every branch, opening time and service you need to know about.

Liverpool City Overview & Main City Centre Post Office

Liverpool is a major port city and commercial hub in merseyside, home to two universities, a thriving tourism scene and a dense residential population that demands reliable postal services. The network of post office branches here is one of the most extensive in the North West.

The main Liverpool ONE Post Office sits at 1–3 south john street, Liverpool, L1 8BN, right in the heart of the shopping district between Church Street and Paradise Street. This central branch offers the widest range of services: mail, parcels, banking, travel money and identity services - including options to apply for passport Check & Send where available.

Nearby city centre branches at Central Village (39 Dawson Way, L1 1LJ), Exchange Street East (L2 3PQ) and Monument Place (83–85 London Road, L3 8JA) cover the L1, L2 and L3 postcode areas. All are within walking distance of landmarks like Liverpool ONE shopping centre, Lime Street station and the waterfront.

Post Offices in Liverpool: How Many Branches & Where They Are

Liverpool has 46 post office branches covering postcode areas from L1 through L37 and surrounding districts. These range from full-size Crown offices in busy shopping areas to smaller counters inside convenience stores and supermarkets.

Well-known locations include Allerton Road (L18), Old Swan (L13), Penny Lane (L15), Waterloo (L22), Kirkby (L32), Huyton (L36) and Speke (L24) - giving solid geographic spread across north, south, east and central Liverpool.

Around 18 locations offer Drop and Collect services, allowing customers to drop off and pick up parcels without waiting for full counter service. A detailed map of all branches is usually available via the official online branch finder website, using mapping tools for easy visual navigation.

Finding a Post Office Near Me in Liverpool

To find your nearest branch, search "Post Office near me Liverpool" or enter a postcode (e.g. L1 8BN, L13 3BU) into the official branch finder. Results typically show each branch's full address, distance and whether services like Drop & Collect, cash withdrawals or travel money are available.

Always check today's opening hours for your chosen branch - times can differ significantly between city centre offices and smaller local branches.

Customers without internet access can often find opening times displayed on the branch door or by phoning the Post Office customer service line. Students near the universities should look for branches around London Road and the L3 area for convenient access.

Services Offered by Liverpool Post Offices

Post offices in Liverpool offer a wide range of essential services well beyond posting letters:

  • Postal services - sending items within the UK and internationally, tracked and signed-for deliveries

  • Drop & Go - Liverpool post offices operate using a Drop & Go fast dispatch service for quick parcel sending

  • Parcelforce Express - specialized courier options like Parcelforce Express are available for urgent deliveries

  • Parcel collection and returns - post offices provide parcel collection and returns for major retailers and couriers

  • Everyday banking - post offices provide everyday banking services for most high-street banks, including cash withdrawals and deposits

  • Bill payments - services include utility bill payments and top-ups for prepay services

  • Travel money - travel money services include foreign currency exchange, plus travel money cards at larger branches

  • Passport services - some locations offer passport-related services, including Check & Send

  • Identity verification - post offices provide identity verification services for selected banks and government providers

Before making a journey, check the branch details page on the official website to confirm whether your chosen Liverpool post office supports the specific service you need.

Online Tools, Security Verification & "Verification Successful" Messages

When you search for "post office Liverpool" on directory websites, you may briefly see a holding page before results load. This is a security service designed to protect branch-finder data from malicious bots and automated scraping.

The page typically runs a background check that verifies you're a real person - not a bot. Technical identifiers like a respond ray id may appear at the bottom of the page. After a few seconds of waiting, a verification successful message is displayed and you're redirected to the full content: maps, addresses and opening times.

This security verification process is routine and doesn't require personal information. Simply avoid refreshing the page repeatedly - let the system complete its checks and load your results.

FAQ – Liverpool Post Offices

Is there a Post Office in Liverpool city centre close to Liverpool ONE and Church Street?

Yes. The main Liverpool city centre post office is at 1–3 south john street, L1 8BN, inside Liverpool ONE - a short walk from Church Street. Additional central branches at Central Village, Exchange Street East and Monument Place on London Road also serve the wider city centre.

Which Liverpool Post Offices open the latest in the evening?

The latest closing times belong to branches inside supermarkets and convenience stores. Some Drop & Collect locations stay open until 9:30pm or even 11pm on weekdays. Check online details for in-store branches in areas like Broadgreen, Speke or Huyton to confirm specific late-night hours.

Are any Liverpool Post Offices open on Sunday?

Around 20–21 branches open on Sundays, generally with shorter hours (often 11am–3pm). These are typically inside shops, so you can combine posting errands with grocery shopping. Always confirm Sunday hours online before you travel.

What should I do if my local Liverpool Post Office is temporarily closed?

Use the online branch finder to locate the next closest branch in your postcode area. Many districts are served by more than one post office. For basic parcel sending, you can buy postage online, print labels at home and drop items at an alternative Drop & Collect location nearby.

Can I do identity or security checks for financial services at Liverpool Post Offices?

Some larger Liverpool post offices support identity verification for selected banks and government services, but availability differs by branch. Check your provider's requirements first, then confirm via the branch details page whether your chosen location offers that specific in-branch verification.
