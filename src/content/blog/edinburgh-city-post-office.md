## Overview of Edinburgh City Post Office (EH1 1BQ)

<p>The edinburgh city post office is the principal central branch for the capital, sitting inside the mall waverley bridge shopping centre - formally known as Waverley Mall. <p>Yes. The old St James Centre / Princes Mall post office has closed, but the edinburgh city post office at waverley mall (Unit 5-6, waverley bridge, eh1 1bq) now serves as the main central branch, just steps from princes street and Waverley Station.</p>

### Which Post Office is best if I am staying near the Royal Mile or Edinburgh Old Town?

<p>The Waverley Mall branch is usually the most convenient. It is a short walk downhill from the Royal Mile via North Bridge or Cockburn Street to waverley bridge - roughly five minutes on foot.</p>

### Can I renew my passport or driving licence at Edinburgh City Post Office?

<p>Edinburgh City Post Office typically offers passport and driving licence photo services, Check &amp; Send and document certification. Confirm exact availability on the official Post Office branch finder before your visit, as some services operate at particular times.</p>

### Are any Edinburgh Post Offices open late or on Sundays?

<p>The central edinburgh city post office keeps standard daytime hours and is closed on Sundays. However, several suburban branches inside supermarkets and convenience stores stay open later on weekdays - some until around 22:00 - and a handful open on Sundays with shorter hours.</p>

### How many Post Office branches are there in Edinburgh city?
