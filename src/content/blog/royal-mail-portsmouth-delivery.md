## Frequently Asked Questions



### Can I buy stamps and pay bills at the Portsmouth Delivery Office?

<p>The Voyager Park South delivery office is mainly set up for collections and prepaid drop-offs. Full counter services such as buying stamps in bulk, paying bills, or accessing banking services are provided at post office branches like the one on Slindon Street at PO1 1AB. If you need to post a new item with postage or handle financial transactions, head to the branch rather than the delivery office.</p>

### What happens if I lose my "Something for you" card?

<p>If you misplace your card, bring valid photo ID and proof of address to the delivery office. Provide staff with your full name and address so they can attempt to locate the item. Collection without the card is at staff discretion for security reasons, and you may experience a longer wait while they verify your details. Where possible, try to note down the reference number from the card as a backup.</p>

### Can someone else collect my parcel from Portsmouth Delivery Office?

<p>In many cases, a nominated person can collect on your behalf. They should bring the original "Something for you" card, a signed permission note where requested, and suitable ID - ideally belonging to the person named on the item, plus their own identification. Royal Mail staff have the final say on whether to release an item to a third party, particularly for high-value or signature-required parcels.</p>

### Is there step-free access for wheelchair users?

<p>Most modern Royal Mail delivery offices, including Portsmouth Voyager Park South, aim to offer step-free access and ramps. However, visitors with specific mobility needs should confirm the exact facilities via Royal Mail's website or by calling ahead. Staff are generally happy to provide assistance on arrival if you let them know what you need.</p>

### How do I find out if my postcode is served by this delivery office?

<p>Use Royal Mail's official "Find a Delivery Office" or "Services Near You" tool. Enter your full postcode, and the system will confirm whether the Portsmouth Delivery Office at PO3 5GG handles your address or if another unit is responsible for your deliveries. This takes less than a minute and prevents unnecessary trips to the wrong location.</p>
