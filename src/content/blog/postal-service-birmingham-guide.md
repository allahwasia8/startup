## Overview of Postal Services in Birmingham

<p>The postal service birmingham network is split across three distinct operations: post office branches handle retail counter services, royal mail delivery offices manage sorting and deliveries, and the parcelforce worldwide depot handles express parcels. As of mid-2026, there are 54 post office branches serving Birmingham and nearby districts such as Sutton Coldfield, Rubery and Wythall.</p><p>Branches range from full high-street offices to smaller counters inside convenience stores like Spar, One Stop and Premier shops. Birmingham's postcode area (B1–B98) stretches from the city centre through suburbs like northfield and Perry Barr out to edge-of-city locations near Redditch and Solihull. Post Office branches handle UK and international posting, Collect+ parcel services, bill payments and everyday banking with major UK banks.</p>

## Frequently Asked Questions about Postal Services in Birmingham



### Is there a main Post Office for Birmingham, or just local branches?

<p>Birmingham does not have a single "head" main Post Office serving all districts. Instead, it relies on a network of 54 branches spread across the city. For city-centre visitors, the branch at 31-33 Union Street or the Jewellery Quarter office on Vyse Street are the most practical. Royal Mail's Birmingham Delivery Offices on St Stephens Street handle mail processing, not retail services, so use local post offices for everyday posting and banking.</p>

### Where do I collect a Royal Mail parcel that could not be delivered?

<p>Undelivered items are taken to the delivery office named on your "Something for you" card. In Birmingham this is often St Stephens Street (B6 4AB), but check the card and track online before travelling. Bring the card and photo ID. Delivery offices open early - often from 07:00 - but close earlier than standard post offices, so plan accordingly.</p>

### Are Birmingham post offices open on Sundays and bank holidays?

<p>22 post office branches in Birmingham open on sundays, usually those inside supermarkets and convenience stores. Hours are typically limited to late morning and early afternoon. Most high-street branches and all royal mail delivery offices close on UK bank holidays. Use the branch finder to filter for sunday opening if weekend posting is essential.</p>

### What is the difference between Royal Mail, the Post Office and Parcelforce Worldwide?

<p>Royal mail collects, transports and delivers letters and most parcels. The post office runs the retail counters where you buy stamps, send items and access financial services. Parcelforce worldwide is Royal Mail's express parcel brand with its own depot near Birmingham at Pavilion Drive (B6 7BB). Many Birmingham post offices accept Parcelforce parcels for drop-off, but specialist or large international shipments may require visiting the depot directly.</p>

### How can I find a late-opening or early-morning post office in Birmingham?

<p>30 post office branches in Birmingham open after 6pm on weekdays - almost all of them are in-store counters inside supermarkets and convenience stores. Use the services near you tool and filter by opening time. Areas like Kings Heath, Chelmsley Wood and Perry Barr often have extended-hour counters. Always double-check closing times on the day you visit, as extended hours can change due to staffing or store refurbishments. TG Jones and similar local retail partners occasionally adjust schedules, so a quick search before you head out saves wasted journeys.</p>
