## FAQ: Post Offices in Glasgow



### Which Post Office is closest to Glasgow Central and Queen Street stations?

<p>There is no post office inside either Glasgow Central Station or Glasgow Queen Street, and none in the core G1–G5 shopping streets. The West End branch is the closest to the city centre, located on Great Western Road approximately 3.8 km away. Maryhill Post Office to the north and Battlefield Road branches to the south are also within a short bus or subway journey. Use a postcode search from the station to find the best branch for your route.</p>

### Are any Glasgow Post Offices open late in the evening?

<p>While most Glasgow branches close by 17:30, there are around two dozen locations that open past 18:00 on at least one weekday. Late-opening branches are typically inside supermarkets and convenience stores such as Tesco Craigmarloch, Nisa Nitshill or Morrisons Cathkin. Filter for "open now" or "late opening" on your branch-finder tool. Service choice may be more limited in the evening, focusing mainly on posting and collecting parcels.</p>

### Can I collect missed parcels from any Post Office in Glasgow?

<p>Missed parcels are normally sent to a specific delivery office or nominated collection point, not to every local post office counter. The Royal Mail card left at your address will state exactly where and when the parcel can be collected, which may be a delivery office like the one on Victoria Road or Fullarton Drive. Some services allow redirection to a participating branch, but this must be arranged online in advance. Always check tracking information before heading out.</p>

### Do Glasgow Post Offices offer Sunday or bank holiday services?

<p>A minority of Glasgow post offices open on Sundays - specifically, 28 post office branches in Glasgow open on Sundays - typically those housed inside supermarkets or larger convenience stores. Sunday hours are shorter than weekdays and mainly cover basic mail and parcel transactions. On UK bank holidays, most standalone branches are closed, while some in-store counters may open with restricted Sunday-style hours. Check individual branch listings before travelling.</p>

### How can I check if a specific Glasgow Post Office is open right now?

<p>The quickest way is to use an online branch finder, searching by postcode or branch name. The results will display today's opening times and whether the branch is currently open. Many tools also flag special hours for Saturdays, Sundays and bank holidays, plus temporary closures. Bookmark your nearest Glasgow branches for easy reference, and call ahead if travelling a long distance since hours can change at short notice.</p>
