## FAQ – Green Lanes &amp; Palmers Green Post Offices



### Is Green Lanes Post Office the same as Palmers Green Post Office?

<p>No. They are two separate branches on the same main road. Green Lanes Post Office is at 822 Green Lanes, Winchmore Hill N21 2RT, while Palmers Green Post Office is at 364 Green Lanes, London N13 5XL. They serve slightly different neighbourhoods, though their catchment areas overlap along the Green Lanes corridor.</p>

### Can I do everyday banking at these Green Lanes branches?

<p>Yes. Many everyday personal banking services are available at both locations, including cash withdrawals, deposits, and balance enquiries for selected UK banks. However, not every bank account is supported and transaction limits may apply - check with your bank or the branch beforehand.</p>

### Is there parking near the Green Lanes and Palmers Green Post Offices?

<p>Street parking is usually available on or just off Green Lanes, often in controlled bays with time limits. Check local signs for restrictions and consider public transport during peak times when finding a space can be difficult.</p>

### What ID do I need for collecting parcels or using banking services?

<p>You generally need valid photo ID (passport or driving licence) plus the collection card or reference number from the delivery carrier. For services like Passport Check &amp; Send, additional documents such as proof of address may be required. Always check the exact requirements on your collection notification.</p>

### Do opening hours change on bank holidays?

<p>Yes. Hours for both Green Lanes and Palmers Green Post Offices often change on UK bank holidays, with some days closed entirely. Always confirm the latest hours via the official Post Office website or by calling the branch before travelling.</p>
